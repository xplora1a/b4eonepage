About Us

Bracknell for Europe campaigns for a People’s Vote on any Brexit Deal with an option to Remain, and for the UK to remain in the European Union.

We have over 100 members in the Bracknell area who campaign on the street, door to door and distribute campaign material. We are affiliated to both Berkshire for Europe and Open Britain Berkshire, and through them to the national campaign bodies.

We meet at least once a month for a social and campaign update where we coordinate our activity.
